About us



M/S Solution is a dedicated legal consultancy firm committed to providing prompt, reliable, and result-oriented legal services. Located in Lucknow, our firm comprises a team of seasoned and specialized advocates offering comprehensive legal support across various domains.

We provide consultancy and assistance in matters related to civil and criminal law, family disputes, D.R.T. cases, labour issues, insurance claims, service matters, company law, trademark and patent registration, customs, banking, RERA compliance, and consumer redressal. Our scope of work also includes registry services, marriage registration, mutation, wills, family counselling, agreements, copyright, partnership matters, NGO and firm registration, and other essential legal processes.

Our legal team includes experienced professionals such as K.K. Gupta, Sanjay Shukla, Abhinay Singh, Padmakar Nath Tripathi, Rekha Rawat, Praveen Srivastava, Harshit Pandey, among others, each possessing in-depth knowledge in their respective fields of practice.

At M/S Solution, we prioritize integrity, efficiency, and client satisfaction. We are committed to delivering well-structured legal advice and services that meet the specific needs of individuals, businesses, and institutions.

Our consultation hours are from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM, allowing flexibility for clients who may require services outside standard working hours.

For dependable legal guidance with a professional approach, M/S Solution stands as a trusted partner in navigating legal complexities.
